COREP C 01.00 Template Instructions

Template C 01.00 - Own Funds Instructions for Reporting

This template is used for reporting the composition of own funds according to the Capital Requirements Regulation (CRR) and PRA Rulebook requirements.

Template Structure:

Section 1: CET1 Capital (Rows 010-040)
- Row 010: Ordinary share capital (including share premium account)
- Row 020: Retained earnings (after tax)
- Row 030: Other disclosed reserves
- Row 040: Other CET1 instruments meeting criteria

Section 2: AT1 Capital (Rows 120-130)
- Row 120: AT1 instruments meeting criteria
- Row 130: Other AT1 items

Section 3: Tier 2 Capital (Rows 200-210)
- Row 200: Tier 2 instruments meeting criteria
- Row 210: Other Tier 2 items

Section 4: Regulatory Deductions (Rows 350-380)
- Row 350: Intangible assets (including goodwill)
- Row 360: Deferred tax assets
- Row 370: Investments in own shares
- Row 380: Other regulatory deductions

Reporting Requirements:

Currency and Units:
- All amounts must be reported in GBP for UK institutions
- Values should be in thousands of GBP (000s)
- Negative values for deductions only
- Zero for non-applicable items

Validation Rules:
1. CET1 capital must be positive before deductions
2. AT1 capital cannot exceed 50% of CET1 capital
3. Tier 2 capital cannot exceed 100% of CET1 capital
4. All deductions must be properly justified
5. Template must reconcile to summary totals

Data Quality Requirements:
- Source documentation must be maintained
- Regulatory references required for each item
- Changes from previous period must be explained
- Data gaps must be identified and documented

Audit Trail Requirements:
- Each populated field must reference regulatory source
- Justification must include specific article or rule reference
- Calculation methodology must be documented
- Changes in accounting treatment must be explained

Common Reporting Issues:

1. Intangible Assets:
   - Goodwill must be fully deducted (Row 350)
   - No partial recognition allowed
   - Must reference CRR Article 36

2. Retained Earnings:
   - Must be after-tax amounts
   - Exclude proposed dividends
   - Reference CRR Article 26(1)(c)

3. AT1 Instruments:
   - Must meet loss absorption criteria
   - Cannot be redeemable at issuer's option
   - Reference CRR Article 51

4. Tier 2 Instruments:
   - Must have minimum 5-year maturity
   - Must be subordinated to senior debt
   - Reference CRR Article 62

Reporting Frequency:
- Quarterly reporting required
- Due within 20 working days of period end
- Must be signed off by senior management
- Subject to regulatory review and validation
